About the Sustainable Futures Resource Fair

Our Goal

The Sustainable Futures Resource Fair is a collaborative initiative designed to bring together local organizations and experts to share information, resources, and actionable steps that inspire patrons to protect their health and the environment. It is a travelling event that will be held at libraries across Delaware.

Why Attend?

The Resource Fair offers a unique opportunity to:

  • Learn about the intersection of environmental and public health
  • Connect with local organizations and experts
  • Discover practical steps for sustainable living
  • Access community resources and support
  • Participate in Delaware's sustainable future
