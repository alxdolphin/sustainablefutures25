2025 Sustainable Futures Resource Fair

Route 9 Library and Innovation Center

March 8, 2025 11:00 AM - 2:00 PM

About

The 2025 Sustainable Futures Resource Fair aims to bring together local organizations and experts to share information, resources, and actionable steps that inspire patrons to protect their health and the environment. We are particularly focused on health aspects closely tied to environmental factors, such as air quality's impact on respiratory health, sustainable food systems and dietary health, and similar topics.
